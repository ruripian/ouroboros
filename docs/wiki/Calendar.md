# Calendar

**English** · [한국어](캘린더)

The Calendar view shows **issues with dates** and **standalone events** side by side on a monthly grid. Switch to it from the tab bar at the top of any project.

![Calendar month view](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ruripian/OrbiTail/main/docs/wiki-screenshots/03-calendar-month.png)

## Issues vs events

- **Issues** show up as thin strips spanning from their start date to their due date. Click one to jump to the issue detail page.
- **Events** are colored blocks for meetings, trips, deadlines, presentations, milestones, and anything else that is not an issue. Events live only on the calendar and timeline — they do not show up in the issue table.

A small dot on the day number tells you how many items land on that day.

## My schedule filter

The **My schedule** toggle at the top of the calendar narrows the view to items that involve you personally.

![My schedule filter](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ruripian/OrbiTail/main/docs/wiki-screenshots/03-calendar-my-schedule.png)

When **My schedule** is active, the calendar shows only:

- Issues where you are an assignee.
- Events where you are a participant.
- Events marked **Project-wide** (because those are relevant to every member).

Flip the toggle off to see everything the project has on the calendar.

## Creating and editing an event

Click an empty day cell to create a new event on that day, or click an existing event to edit it. Either way you land on the same dialog.

![Event dialog](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ruripian/OrbiTail/main/docs/wiki-screenshots/03-event-dialog.png)

You can set:

- **Title** — required.
- **Start date / End date** — same day for a one-off, different days for a multi-day event.
- **Type** — Meeting, Trip, Deadline, Presentation, Milestone, or Other. Picking a type suggests a default color.
- **Color** — a palette of six event colors. Pick one to match your team's convention.
- **Participants** — workspace members who are directly involved.
- **Project-wide event** — when this toggle is on, every project member sees the event in **My schedule**, regardless of participants. Turn it off for a focused meeting where only the listed participants care.
- **Description** — free-form text.

Click **Save** to update an existing event, **Create** for a new one, or **Delete** to remove it.
