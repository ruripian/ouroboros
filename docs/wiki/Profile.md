# Profile

**English** · [한국어](프로필)

Your personal settings live under the avatar menu in the top right of every page. Click **Profile Settings** to land on the profile editor.

![Profile page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ruripian/OrbiTail/main/docs/wiki-screenshots/09-profile-page.png?v=2)

The left rail splits your settings into sections:

- **My Account**
  - **Profile** — your name, display name, and email address. Email changes require confirmation via a link sent to the new address.
  - **Preferences** — language (English or Korean), theme (System, Light, or Dark), timezone, and first day of the week.
  - **Security** — change password, view active sessions, log out everywhere.
- **Workspace**
  - **Members** — workspace-wide membership list (admins only).
- **Administration**
  - **User Management** — approve new signups, grant staff status (staff only).
- **Existing this project**
  - Shortcuts to leave or archive the current project.

## Language

OrbiTail ships with English and Korean translations. The language you pick here is saved on the server and applies across every device you log in from. You can switch languages at any time without reloading — the UI updates live.

## Theme

The **System** theme follows your OS preference and reacts when you toggle dark mode in the operating system. **Light** and **Dark** are pinned regardless of OS setting. All themes share the same accent color palette.

## Editing your name

**First Name** and **Last Name** are used by notification emails and activity feeds. **Display Name** is what the rest of the workspace sees next to your avatar. Changing any of them is instant — click **Save** and your avatar initials update in every open tab.
